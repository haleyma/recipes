Three cans DelMonte tomatoes, with mild green chilis

One can black beans

One can garbanzo beans/chick peas

1 TBsp Oil
three garlic cloves
One shallot

One large chicken breast - cut into cubes

One packet of Tex Mex Chili seasoning

Shredded sharp cheddar cheese

In skillet, heat oil and saute garlic and shallot.  when shallot's translucent, add chicken and cook until just browned.

Add chilli seasoning.

Add beans

Add tomatoes and simmer until sauce thickens.  Serve over shredded cabbage, or rice, or in bowel.  Top with cheese
